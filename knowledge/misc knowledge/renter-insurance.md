# Renter Insurance

Details on renter's insurance for their property and what is required

## Policy Requirements

Renter's policies that meet Ziprent's lease requirements must have the following (section 11 of the lease):

### Coverage Requirements

Tenants choice. Per lease: "Owner requires Tenant to hold a Renter's Insurance Policy with Tenant's choice of benefits and coverage, see state law addendum for specific requirements, if any. Tenant acknowledges that Owner's insurance does not cover Tenant's personal property."

## Management Services & Tenant Placement

Lease requirements for renter's insurance is always applicable. Regardless of whether a property is fully managed or simply using tenant placement services, the lease will still requires tenants to have renters insurance.

*Ziprent will only continue to followup for renter's insurance for managed properties. For tenant placement, our services end once a tenant is placed and the lease is signed (everything else is the owners responsibility at that point).*

## Additional Insured vs Additional Interest

*We are NOT currently enforcing the requirements for tenants to list Ziprent as Additional Insured. But we may in the future. Managers can ACCEPT Renter's Policies that do not list Ziprent.*

When it comes to insurance, an Additional Interest is an entity with a financial interest in the property that's being insured. An Additional Insured, on the other hand, is an entity that is also covered by the insurance policy purchased. This is why Ziprent must be listed as Additional Insured, not Additional Interest.

## Additional Insured Details

*Reference chart at bottom of page*

*We are NOT currently enforcing the requirements for tenants to list Ziprent as Additional Insured. But we may in the future. Managers can ACCEPT Renter's Policies that do not list Ziprent.*

Every insurer is different, every policy uses unique formatting and declaration page verbiages vary for the same coverages. The chart at the bottom of this page lists insurance companies and how they provide the Additional Insured coverage that meets Ziprent's lease requirements.

### Policy Verbiage

- **Additional Insured** — Ziprent is covered by the insurance policy
- **Additional Interest (coverage)** — While Additional Interest normally does not mean that Ziprent is covered, some companies do provide coverage if it says Additional Interest on the forms
- **Automatically Covered** — This means Ziprent is covered without specifically saying we are on the policy; the policy just covers us by default
- **Other Language that provides Ziprent coverage** — Secondary Insured, Additional Named Insured, Insured, Second Named Insured
- **Does not Support** — This means that the insurance company will not cover Ziprent in any way, Additional Interest can also fall under this description if Ziprent will not be covered by the policy, but only listed for notifications. THEY HAVE TO GET A NEW POLICY TO WORK WITH US
