## ACH Process Times

A breakdown of timelines for Rent or paid Invoices through the Ziprent Portal:

### Timeline Breakdown

| Step | Description | Timeline |
|------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Initiate: Invoice added / Rent Paid at Ziprent | Day 1 |
| 2 | Debit: Money taken from paying account | Next Business Day |
| 3 | Credit: Money arrives in the receives account | 2 Business Days later |

**Example Timeline:**
- Monday: Tenant pays rent
- Tuesday: Money is debited from tenant's account
- Wednesday: Money arrives in owner's account

When a tenant pays before 5pm, it takes 2 business days to process the funds. So if a tenant paid on a Monday, the funds would appear in the account on Wednesday.

### Important Processing Details

In order to process this in a timely manner we have to submit both the debit and credit of the transaction at the same time. Once this process starts it is impossible to stop and can only be corrected by processing another transaction. (We do have some regulations we also need to handle so we can't put a hold on the funds for an extended period of time.)

### Bounce Handling

Should a tenant rent payment bounce, we learn about it on Thursday (2 days after they initiated their rent) and one day before the receiver's account is credited. As such, in order to correctly account for the funds, we need to process the transaction right away. So in general, for owners, what should happen is the credit from the first transaction applies to the account first and then the debit would come out. Unfortunately that transaction depends on the bank and sometimes they delay the debit.

**Important Note:** Regardless of what the tenant sees on their side with a payment clearing/bouncing, we HAVE to wait for our bank to report the issue before anything can happen.

**Do not tell the tenants/owners this:** but the bank only reports failures, not clears.

### Clearing Details

| Step | Description | Timeline |
|------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Initiate: Invoice added / Rent Paid at Ziprent | Day 1 |
| 2 | Debit: Clears the debit of the account | 3 Business Days |
| 3 | Credit: Clears the credit into the account | 4 Business Days later |
