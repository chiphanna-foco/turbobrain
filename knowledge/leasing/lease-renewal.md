# Lease Renewal Process

## When to Renew

Lease renewal notices are sent 60 days before your lease expires. You have 30 days to respond.

## Renewal Options

1. **Renew for 12 months** - Best rate, typically 3-5% increase from current rent
2. **Renew for 6 months** - Moderate rate, 5-8% increase
3. **Month-to-month** - Highest rate, 10-15% increase

## How to Renew

1. Log into your resident portal at portal.ziprent.com
2. Go to "My Lease" > "Renewal Options"
3. Select your preferred term
4. Sign electronically
5. Your new lease will be emailed within 24 hours

## Questions About Renewal Pricing

Contact our leasing team at leasing@ziprent.com or call (555) 123-4567.

## Moving Out Instead

If you don't want to renew:
- Provide written notice 30 days before your lease ends
- Schedule a move-out inspection
- Return all keys on your move-out date
- Security deposit will be refunded within 21 days after move-out inspection
