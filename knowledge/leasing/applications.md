# Leasing: Applications | Ziprent Wiki

## Overview

**Applications: The "Processing Machine"**

The Applications Team processes all tenant applications against qualification criteria and presents them to owners. Fair Housing compliance is critical—applicants must receive equal opportunity regardless of protected characteristics.

## Goals

- Complete and process applications within 30 minutes of receipt
- Follow all Fair Housing requirements
- Reach out within 24 hours if application incomplete
- Measure all applications against qualification criteria
- Present all qualified applications (even if partial qualification)

## Qualification Criteria

**Income**: 3x monthly rent in combined gross income

**Credit**: 650+ credit score (all adult applicants)

**Background**: 
- Clear of liens
- No bankruptcies
- No evictions
- No judgments
- No fraudulent activity
- Clear criminal record

**Other Requirements**
- Non-expired photo ID (Driver's License, passport)
- Application fee: $40 (refundable upon request)
- Consent to SMS, email, phone follow-ups

## Application States

**PENDING REVIEW**: Submitted minimum documents
- All adult applicants completed application
- IDs attached
- At least one pay stub/bank statement attached

**INCOMPLETE**: Missing essential information
- Additional applicants needed
- Documents not uploaded
- Email addresses incorrect for occupants

**COMPLETED**: Income verified, all documents reviewed
- Manual income verification complete for all applicants
- Ready to propose, pin, or decline

**PROPOSED**: Sent to owner for approval
- Background checks automatically running
- Awaiting owner decision

## Income Verification (In Order of Importance)

1. **TrueWorks** (most accurate)
   - Works with payroll
   - Shows hourly/salary income
   - Verifies employment
   - Reflects gross income

2. **Ocrolus + Pay Stubs + Job Offer Letter**
   - Go by gross amount
   - Only need one for salary/full-time
   - Need 2-3 for variable hourly

3. **Closing Docs** (newer software)
   - Works with bank accounts
   - Verifies account funds
   - Reflects net income (may be lower)
   - Note: Doesn't account for new jobs; sometimes wrong amount
   - Use: 12-Month Average + 50% Non-Recurring Income

4. **6 Months Bank Statements**
   - For self-employed, cash earners
   - For multiple income streams
   - Review deposits for income average
   - Check withdrawals and balances for savings

**Red Flags**: Deposits are just couple hundred dollars or account goes negative

## Calculating Income from Pay Stubs

**Salary (Straightforward)**
- Bi-weekly: multiply by 26, divide by 12
- Weekly: multiply by 52, divide by 12
- Monthly: that's the gross monthly income

**Hourly (Variable)**
- Find hourly rate and hours worked
- $20/hour × 40 hours/week = $800/week × 52 ÷ 12 = $3,467/month
- Or: $20 × 2 = $40k/year ÷ 12 = $3,333/month
- Need 2-3 pay stubs if hours vary each period
- Average hours, then multiply by rate

**Tips/Commission**
- Ask for 6 months bank statements
- Can average from multiple pay stubs
- Add to hourly base

**Self-Employed**
- Ask for 6 months business bank statements
- Average monthly income from deposits
- Use Closing Docs software

## Incomplete Applications

**Common Reasons**
- Additional occupants missing
- Payment not made
- Documents not uploaded
- Email addresses incorrect for occupants

**How to Fix**
1. Check entire application
2. Are additional applicants linked? (Check Ziprent)
3. If unlinked: find main applicant, match email, save, refresh
4. Do all have non-expired IDs?
5. Does one have income attached?
6. Use Follow Up button in Ziprent
7. Archive thread

**Send Follow-Up**
- Hello (Person's Name)
- Request specific documents
- Include personalized link to re-access
- Or: email to applications@ziprent.com
- Merge any emailed documents to application thread

## Pending Review Application Issues

**Frozen Credit**
- Use Follow Up button in Ziprent
- Ask applicant to unfreeze for soft credit check
- Once unfrozen: click Init. Credit, CRS Credit
- Should auto-update

**Missing Recent Pay Stub**
- Push to verify through TrueWorks/Ocrolus first
- If pushback: accept manual verification
- Use Follow Up button if needed

**Missing Documents**
- Use Follow Up feature
- Request specific documents
